Nerdish? Ladyish? Fancy a social hour (or two) with other nerdy ladies? Then head on down to Joystick Gamebar every first Thursday at 7pm.

You'll find members from Women Who Code, Rails Girls Atlanta, and PyLadiesAtl who range from full time software developers to those simply curious about programming/development/technology in general (and everyone in between).
